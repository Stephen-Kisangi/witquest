Nurturing Excellence Since 2023

FAQS Academic Champs Our Pride

Established in 2023, Witquest Castle School implements the Competency-Based Curriculum through modern smart classrooms and innovative learning spaces. Our child-first approach blends competency-focused learning with cutting-edge technology, nurturing every learner's potential.

Aligned with CBC pillars, our STEM, arts, and citizenship programs thrive in an inspiring castle environment where curriculum excellence meets 21st-century innovation for holistic skill development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school

Witquest Castle School follows the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) as mandated by the Ministry of Education in Kenya. Our implementation includes enhanced STEM integration, arts education, and global citizenship components that extend beyond the basic requirements.

We provide education for children from Pre-Primary 1 (PP1) through Grade 6, with plans to expand to all junior secondary levels by 2026. Our early childhood education center serves children from 3 years of age.

Our castle theme creates an immersive learning environment that sparks imagination and curiosity. Learning spaces are designed as themed "realms" of knowledge, encouraging exploration and discovery. The castle architecture provides unique reading nooks, science laboratories, and creative spaces that make learning magical.

We offer a rich array of activities including sports (football, swimming, athletics), performing arts (music, dance, drama), creative arts, coding club, robotics, chess club, environmental club, and community service initiatives. Each student is encouraged to participate in at least two extra-curricular activities.
