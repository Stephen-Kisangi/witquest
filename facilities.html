Our World-Class Facilities

At Witquest Castle, we provide exceptional learning environments that support holistic development and inspire academic excellence.

Study Room

Quiet, comfortable spaces designed for focused individual learning and group study sessions with modern furniture and resources.

Creativity Space

Innovative areas where students can explore their artistic abilities and engage in creative thinking and problem-solving.

Featured

Laboratory

Well-equipped science labs with the latest equipment to conduct experiments and develop practical scientific knowledge.

Sport Field

Expansive outdoor sports fields for football, athletics, and various team sports, promoting physical fitness and teamwork.

Dining Room

Spacious, hygienic dining facilities serving nutritious meals prepared by professional chefs with a focus on balanced nutrition.

Auditorium

Modern auditorium with excellent acoustics and lighting for school performances, assemblies, and cultural events.

Gymnastic Rooms

Dedicated spaces with specialized equipment for gymnastics training, developing balance, flexibility, and coordination.

Featured

Swimming Pool

Olympic-sized swimming pool with trained instructors for swimming lessons and competitive training in a safe environment.

School Library

Extensive collection of books, digital resources, and comfortable reading areas to foster a love for reading and research.

Music Room

Sound-proof music rooms equipped with various instruments for music education, practice, and performances.
